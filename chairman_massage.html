Chairman's Message

Bangladesh is the home of jute and it is our privilege that we are trying to establish the name of jute at its rightful place among natural fibers in the world. Jute is environment friendly, biodegradable and is not a threat to human health.

I thank everyone who use jute & jute products because you are not only saving nature; you are also helping millions to grow. I appreciate the constant support from our valued customers & my dedicated colleagues who have inspired me to grow & build a better tomorrow.
If you decide to use jute yarn then AKIJ is the name on which you can depend because this very name stands for quality.
Thanks to all of you.


Sheikh Nasir Uddin (CIP)
